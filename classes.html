Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Gentle Hatha Flow Yoga Sequence For Better Nervous System Gentle yoga is ideal for your students who want to progress slowly, but want to increase flexibility, and focus on breathing. Gentle yoga comprises selected poses that flow in a structured way without straining the stretched muscles and joints.While practicing gentle yoga the body movements are in deep connection with the breath. The practice focuses on getting the body in the pose and then remaining in the posture for a stipulated period. Gentle yoga is extremely helpful in opening the muscles around the organs without straining or overstretching them.
Vinyasa Yoga
Vinyasa yoga, also called "flow" because of the smooth way the poses run together, is one of the most popular contemporary styles of yoga. It's a broad classification that encompasses many different types of yoga – Ashtanga and power yoga.
Restorative Yoga
Restorative yoga is a restful practice that is all about slowing down and opening your body through passive stretching. If you take a restorative class, you may hardly move at all, doing just a few postures over the course of an hour. Restorative yoga is a completely different experience than most other styles of yoga. Learn about what restorative yoga is and why it's important to slow down and embrace the power of rest.
